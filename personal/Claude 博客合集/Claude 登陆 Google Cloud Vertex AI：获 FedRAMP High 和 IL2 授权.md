Today, we're announcing that Claude models are now authorized for FedRAMP High and IL-2 workloads through Google Cloud's Vertex AI platform.

With these authorizations, federal civilian agencies and defense organizations can use Claude while meeting their strict regulatory compliance requirements, helping balance innovation and security.

### Expanding government access to Claude

The Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program (FedRAMP) provides a standardized approach to security assessment for federal cloud services. Similarly, Defense contractors and agencies can use Claude within environments authorized up to DoD Impact Level 2 (IL2).

### Clearing the way for government AI innovation

Claude on Vertex AI offers fully managed and serverless models as APIs. Claude's FedRAMP High and IL2 availability marks significant progress for government customers and lays groundwork toward future IL5 compatibility.
